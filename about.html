About The Website

Learn more about the purpose of the Qatar Airways Fleet Wallpaper website and its connection to the aviation fan community.

Website Purpose

This website was created to serve as a dedicated hub for fans of Qatar Airways to easily browse and download high-definition wallpapers of the airline's aircraft. The goal is to provide a structured and organized platform that goes beyond what is currently available through social media alone.

The website focuses on showcasing the full Qatar Airways fleet, from the short-haul Airbus A320 family to the iconic Boeing 777 and the flagship Airbus A350. Users can browse by aircraft type, preview wallpapers in high resolution and go through a simulated download process designed for academic demonstration purposes.

Community Background

This website was inspired by the qatarairwayslovers aviation fan community on Instagram. The community was built around a shared passion for Qatar Airways, its aircraft and the broader aviation world. Over time it grew into a dedicated space where aviation enthusiasts could share photography, updates and appreciation for one of the world's most recognized airlines.

The idea behind this website is to take that community spirit and bring it into a more structured digital platform. Rather than relying purely on Instagram posts, users can now access a clean and organized gallery of wallpapers organized by aircraft type, making it easier to find and download the content they love.

About The Creator

This website was created by Letso Allen Maphorisa, a first-year Business Intelligence and Data Analytics student at Botswana Accountancy College, as part of the Web and Multimedia Development module assignment. Beyond academics, Letso is widely known within the aviation community as the founder and manager of the qatarairwayslovers fan page on Instagram.

The community was started in July 2021 during the COVID-19 pandemic, when Letso was only 14 years old. What began as a personal passion for aviation and Qatar Airways gradually grew into one of the largest Qatar Airways fan communities in the world, attracting more than 30,000 followers globally.

This project represents the combination of academic web development skills with a real-world aviation community that already exists online. The website was designed to provide aviation enthusiasts with a more organized and immersive platform for exploring high-quality Qatar Airways fleet wallpapers while also showcasing the creator's long-term passion for aviation and future ambition of becoming a pilot.
